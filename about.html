About

Better app is an all in one app that was created by two IGIRE Rwanda students
(Eddy, Marquise) located in Rwanda.

Our essay generator feature uses advanced algorithms and natural language processing technology to generate unique, plagiarism-free essays on any topic.

Our email generator feature helps you write professional and effective emails quickly.
Whether you need to send a business proposal, a job application, or a follow-up email,our email generator feature will help you compose the perfect email with ease.

our grammar correction feature is designed to catch grammar and spelling mistakes in your writing.
The app analyzes your text and provides real-time suggestions for corrections, making sure that your writing is error-free and easy to read.

With all these features combined in one app, you'll be able to write faster, more efficiently, and with more accuracy than ever before.
